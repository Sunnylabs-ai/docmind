# Aurora Coffee — Our Story

Aurora Coffee was founded in 2017 by Mara Ellison in Halifax, Nova Scotia. The first shop opened inside a decommissioned lighthouse on the harbor, which the city had scheduled for demolition. Mara bought it at auction for one dollar, on the condition that she restore the lamp room — which now hosts our monthly poetry nights.

The company roasts all its beans in a converted fishing warehouse two blocks from the original lighthouse. We source exclusively from three family farms: two in Huila, Colombia and one in Sidama, Ethiopia.

In 2022, Aurora opened its second location, Harborview, which doubles as the company office. A third location in Dartmouth is planned for next year.

The name "Aurora" comes from the northern lights, which Mara watched from the lighthouse roof the night she signed the purchase papers.
