# Aurora Coffee — Employee Handbook

## Vacation policy

Every full-time employee at Aurora Coffee receives 23 paid vacation days per year, plus your birthday off. Unused days do not roll over past March 31 of the following year. Requests longer than one week must be approved by your shift lead at least 14 days in advance.

## Remote work

Baristas obviously work on-site, but office staff (accounting, marketing, supply chain) may work remotely on Tuesdays and Thursdays. All-hands meetings happen in person on the first Monday of each month at the Harborview location.

## Equipment

Our flagship espresso machine is a 1987 La Marzocco affectionately named Bertha. Bertha receives mandatory maintenance every Friday at 6:00 AM before opening. Only staff who have completed the Level 2 Espresso Certification may operate her.

## Dogs

Dogs are welcome on the patio but not inside, with one exception: Biscuit, the founder's golden retriever, is allowed everywhere including the roastery.
