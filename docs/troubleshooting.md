# Nimbus T2 — Troubleshooting

## Thermostat shows as offline in the app

First, confirm your router's 2.4 GHz band is enabled — the T2 cannot see 5 GHz networks. If the network is fine, restart the thermostat by pressing and holding the dial for 10 seconds until the screen goes dark, then release. The T2 reconnects automatically within a minute of rebooting.

## Flashing amber light

A flashing amber light means the thermostat is not receiving enough power, usually because there is no C-wire connected. Install the included Nimbus Power Bridge at your furnace control board, or have an electrician verify the C-wire connection. Running on insufficient power drains the internal battery and causes random reboots.

## Blank screen

Check the HVAC breaker — a tripped breaker is the most common cause. If the breaker is on, remove the display from the baseplate and check that the wire terminals are seated. If the screen stays blank after reseating, contact support for a replacement under warranty.

## Firmware update stuck

Firmware updates normally take up to 15 minutes. If the progress bar hasn't moved for more than 30 minutes, restart the thermostat with the 10-second dial hold — the T2 resumes the update safely from where it stopped. Never cut power at the breaker during an update.
