# Nimbus — Plans and Billing

## Plans

Nimbus Basic is free forever and includes scheduling, remote control from the app, and safety temperature alerts. Nimbus Plus costs $4.99 per month (or $49 per year, two months free) and adds monthly energy reports, multi-home support, humidity automation, and priority support with a 4-hour response target.

## Managing your subscription

You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime in the Nimbus app under Settings → Subscription. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period — you keep Plus features until then. Annual plans canceled within the first 30 days receive a prorated refund.

## Payment issues

If a payment fails, Nimbus retries after 3 days and sends you an email. Plus features pause after two failed attempts and resume automatically once payment succeeds. We accept credit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay; we do not accept PayPal.
