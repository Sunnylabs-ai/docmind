# Aurora Coffee — Signature Menu

## Midnight Fog — $6.25

Our best seller. Cold brew steeped for 18 hours, layered with lavender oat milk foam and a pinch of smoked sea salt. Available only after 2 PM, because the cold brew batch from the morning needs the full steep.

## The Lighthouse — $5.50

A double cortado pulled on Bertha, served with a shortbread cookie shaped like a lighthouse. Named after our first shop.

## Solar Flare — $7.00

Espresso, blood orange syrup made in-house every Sunday, and sparkling water over ice. Seasonal: available April through September only.

## Fog Cutter Tea — $4.75

For the non-coffee crowd: Nova Scotia wild blueberry tea with maple syrup harvested from the founder's family farm in Cape Breton.
