# Nimbus T2 Thermostat — Setup Guide

## Before you install

Turn off power to your HVAC system at the breaker before removing your old thermostat. The Nimbus T2 requires a C-wire (common wire) for continuous power. If your home doesn't have a C-wire, use the Nimbus Power Bridge included in the box — it installs at your furnace control board in about 10 minutes.

## Installing the thermostat

Remove your old thermostat's faceplate and label the wires with the stickers provided. Mount the Nimbus baseplate, connect the labeled wires to the matching terminals, and click the T2 display onto the baseplate. Restore power at the breaker; the Nimbus logo appears within 30 seconds.

## Connecting to Wi-Fi

The Nimbus T2 supports 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi networks only — it will not detect 5 GHz networks. Open the Nimbus app, choose "Add device," and enter the 6-digit pairing code shown on the thermostat display. Pairing typically completes in under two minutes.
