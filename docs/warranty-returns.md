# Nimbus — Warranty and Returns

## Returns

You can return a Nimbus device for any reason within 45 days of delivery for a full refund. Return shipping is free in Canada and the United States — start the process in the Nimbus app under Settings → Support → Returns, and we'll email a prepaid label. Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5 business days of the device arriving at our warehouse.

## Warranty

Every Nimbus T2 includes a 3-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects, display failures, and sensor drift. The warranty does not cover water damage, damage from unauthorized repairs, or installation outside the supported voltage range. Warranty claims are handled through the app with your device serial number — approved claims ship a replacement unit within 2 business days, before you send the old unit back.

## Out-of-warranty repairs

For devices outside the warranty window, Nimbus offers a flat-fee replacement program: $59 for a refurbished T2 with a fresh 1-year warranty. Trade-in of the old unit is required.
